KAODINMMA KANYINAEME FRIENDS CLUB

Friends club with membership consisting of friends and their immediate families who migrated to the Kwara state from eastern part of Nigeria.

Club Members

OBJECTIVES OF THE CLUB

To promote unity among members and in the society.
To foster the zeal and sprit of oneness, fraternal co-operation and mutual understanding among members
To enhance discipline and order in the lives and activities of the members.

OUR MISSION

Kaodinmma Kanyinaeme Friends Club, Inc. was established to accomplish the following mission:

>To foster interactions between individuals who resides in Ilorin Nigeria.

>To create opportunities for socio-economic networking and development among members.

WHO WE ARE
